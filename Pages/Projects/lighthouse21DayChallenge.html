21 DAY JAVASCRIPT CHALLENGE
--

May 2019 Lighthouse Labs put on a 21 day JavaScript challenge. Over the course of the month it built skills fundamental for a developer using JavaScript. Sadly, due to technical issues on their sever the 21st days challenge was not posted.

The Web Dev Club at the University of Victoria competed in this challenge as a team and ranked in the top 8 at the end of the competition. It was a great experience and would recommend it to anyone looking to step into JavaScript with some past coding knowledge.
